Who We Are

ShieldUp raises awareness about sexual health, safe sex practices, and preventive tools like dental dams and condoms worldwide. Our goal is to educate, inform, and empower individuals with the knowledge to protect themselves and their partners.

Through this platform, users can access detailed information on common STIs, learn how to use protective tools, debunk myths, and discover local resources and clinics. We aim to create a safe space for learning and discussion.

Why ShieldUp?

Sexual health is often overlooked or stigmatized in many communities. ShieldUp provides accurate, culturally relevant, and accessible information to break the taboo and encourage safer practices.

How to Stay Safe

Using barrier methods like condoms and dental dams, regular testing, vaccination (HPV), and open communication with partners are key steps to reduce risks of STIs.

Quick Tips

  • Always use a condom or dental dam during sexual activity.
  • Get tested regularly for STIs.
  • Communicate openly with your partner about sexual health.
  • Educate yourself from reliable sources like WHO, CDC, and Planned Parenthood.
