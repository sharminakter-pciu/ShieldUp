About ShieldUp

ShieldUp is an initiative to raise awareness about sexual health, STIs, and safer intimacy practices in Bangladesh and worldwide.

Our goal is to provide accurate, easy-to-understand information, empowering individuals to make informed decisions and protect their health.

We cover topics like STIs, protective tools like condoms and dental dams, and reliable resources to educate and support our audience.
