Safe Sex Tools

Learn how to use protective tools like dental dams, condoms, and lubricants.

Dental Dam

Thin barrier for oral sex. Prevents STI transmission. Use once and dispose.

Condom

Protection for vaginal, anal, and oral sex. Use correctly every time.

Lubricants

Water or silicone-based. Reduces friction and improves comfort during use of barriers.
