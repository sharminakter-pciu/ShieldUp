STIs Awareness

Learn about sexually transmitted infections and how to protect yourself.

Common STIs

Herpes

Caused by HSV virus. Spread via skin-to-skin contact. Use barrier methods for protection.

HPV

Human Papillomavirus. Vaccination recommended. Barrier methods reduce risk.

Gonorrhea

Bacterial infection. Screening & early treatment is key. Use condoms or dental dams.

Chlamydia

Often asymptomatic. Regular testing advised. Prevent with barrier protection.
